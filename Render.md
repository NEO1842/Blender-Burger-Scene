# 🍔 Blender Burger Scene

A complete Blender scene featuring a stylized 3D burger presentation designed for rendering, lighting practice, material study, and portfolio shots.

---

# ✨ Features

- Detailed burger model
- Procedural materials
- Ready-to-render scene setup
- Adjustable camera composition
- Studio-style lighting
- Beginner-friendly project structure

---

# 🖥 Recommended Blender Version

Blender 3.4 or newer

---

# 🎨 Included Elements

- Burger Bun
- Lettuce
- Tomato
- Cheese
- Patty
- Plate
- Lighting Rig
- Camera Setup

---

# 🚀 Quick Start

1. Open the .blend file.
2. Press **F12** to render.
3. Adjust camera angle if desired.
4. Customize ingredients, colors, and materials.
5. Save your own burger variation.

---

# ⚙ Render Suggestions

Render Engine: Cycles or Eevee

Resolution: 1920 × 1080

Samples:
- Eevee: Default
- Cycles: 128–512

Denoising: Optional

---

# 🍟 Customization Ideas

- Create double burgers
- Add fries and drinks
- Change bun colors
- Build a restaurant scene
- Create advertising renders

---

# 📷 Camera Tips

Lower camera angles often make food appear larger and more appetizing.

Try small Depth of Field values for cinematic close-up shots.
